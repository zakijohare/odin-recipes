Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 tins chopped tomato
  • 2 onions
  • 2 scotch bonnet peppers or 5 green chillis
  • half tub tomato puree
  • tablespoon garlic paste
  • tablespoon ginger paste
  • sunflower oil
  • seasonings below
  • basil
  • oregano
  • salt
  • garlic granules
  • chilli seeds
  • paprika

Instructions

  1. Finely chop onions and chillis. Shallow fry on low heat till onions are brown
  2. Add garlic paste, ginger paste and tomato puree and mix
  4. Add chopped tomatos and let simmer
  5. Add seasonings to taste
  6. Boil pasta in salt water, plate up and enjoy!
